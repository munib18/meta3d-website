What We Do

Our Services

A complete range of 3D printing and additive manufacturing services — from single parts to recurring production runs.

01

Functional Prototyping

Turn your CAD designs into physical, testable prototypes fast. We print parts with the right material for your application — fit testing, functional validation, or client presentations.

02

Low-Volume Batch Production

Need 10, 50, or 100 identical parts? We handle batch runs with consistent quality across every piece — ideal for product sellers, brands, and recurring manufacturing needs.

03

Custom Part Manufacturing

Brackets, fixtures, jigs, mounts, casings, and any custom mechanical or structural component. We manufacture to your exact specifications in the right material.

04

Scale Models & Display Pieces

High-detail scale models, miniatures, display pieces, and souvenirs. Specialising in complex multi-part assemblies with exceptional surface quality and fine detail reproduction.

05

Multicolor FDM Printing

Full multicolor printing for parts that need colour without post-processing. Perfect for branded components, colour-coded assemblies, and consumer products.

06

Resin (SLA) Printing

High-precision resin printing for ultra-fine detail parts, dental models, jewellery masters, and any application requiring surface quality beyond FDM capability.

07

Outsourced Manufacturing Partner

A trusted behind-the-scenes manufacturing partner for designers, agencies, and product consultants. We handle your clients' orders professionally and discreetly.
